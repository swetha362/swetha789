Shanmukha's cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoo s yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour
    •

    For the filling:

    • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
    • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 cup butter

    Instrucions

    1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs
    2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
    3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
    4. Transform the dough into a large boul and cover with a plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
    5. After th dough has double in size,roll it out into a large rectangle
    6. cut the roll into 3cm
